01 — The Problem

The personal shopping industry
is broken and unorganised.

01

No dedicated platform

Thousands of personal shoppers, stylists and vintage hunters advertise themselves on Instagram and TikTok — with no booking system, no payment protection, no trust layer. Clients wire money to strangers and hope for the best.

02

Access is everything

A woman in Warsaw sees the new Chanel Blazy collection go viral on TikTok. It's not available online. The Paris boutique doesn't ship internationally. She has no way to get it — unless she knows someone who knows someone.

03

Millions of items hidden

Hundreds of thousands of vintage stores worldwide never upload their inventory online. Their best pieces sit unseen in cities across Europe while buyers search in vain on Vestiaire and Depop for exactly those pieces.

02 — The Solution
FINDE is a three-sided marketplace connecting clients who want hard-to-find fashion with verified shoppers who have boutique access, and vintage stores whose inventory has never been seen online.
👜
Side one

Clients

Post a sourcing request with a photo, budget and sizes. Browse a live trend feed of what shoppers are seeing in stores right now. Pay only when the item is found and approved — full escrow protection.

✈️
Side two

Personal shoppers

Create a verified profile with boutique access, brand specialities and locations covered. Post videos and photos from inside stores. Receive matched client requests. Earn a sourcing fee on every completed job.

🏪
Side three

Vintage stores

Snap and upload stock on a phone. AI identifies the item automatically. When a client requests something matching your inventory — you're notified instantly. No e-commerce setup required. Just respond and ship.

04 — The Product

Built around the moment
everything clicks.

01

Snap & source

A client uploads any photo — screenshot, TikTok still, magazine page. FINDE's image AI identifies the brand, item and era instantly, matches it against open shopper offers and vintage store inventory simultaneously, and pre-fills the sourcing request automatically.

Image recognition AI

The result: posting a sourcing request takes under 60 seconds. The client's sizes, budget and preferences are auto-filled from their profile. Shoppers see a perfectly briefed job, not a vague DM.

02

Photo approval
before payment

The shopper finds the item, photographs it in the boutique — front, back, hardware, serial number, receipt. The client approves the exact piece before any money moves. Only on approval is the card charged. Funds go into FINDE's escrow — not the shopper — until delivery is confirmed.

Stripe Connect escrow

This is the trust engine that makes FINDE possible. Clients never pay for something they haven't seen. Shoppers never ship without confirmed payment. Both parties are protected by design, not by trust.

03

The live
trend feed

Shoppers post videos and photos from inside boutiques, fashion fairs, and vintage stores in real time. Clients browse a TikTok-style feed of what's actually available in stores right now — not just what brands choose to put online. Every post has a one-tap "Request this" button.

Content-to-commerce pipeline

This is the acquisition engine. Most clients won't come to FINDE knowing what they want — they'll come because they saw something beautiful and realised they could actually get it. The feed creates demand that the marketplace immediately captures.

04

ID verified
shoppers

Every shopper on FINDE is identity verified via government ID, selfie match, and linked bank account before their first job. Their boutique access, brand specialities, and covered locations are listed on their profile. Clients can see SA relationships, member client status, and sourcing track record.

Stripe Identity verification

"Every shopper on FINDE is identity-verified" is a sentence that appears on the homepage and is worth a great deal in a market where people currently wire money to Instagram strangers with zero recourse.

06 — Business Model

Three revenue streams.
All aligned with value delivered.

12%
Transaction commission

Charged on every completed transaction. Split roughly 8% from shopper's sourcing fee and 4% service fee from client. The core revenue engine — only charged when both parties are satisfied. A €4,000 Chanel sourcing job generates €480 in commission from one transaction.

€19
Shopper Pro · per month

Shoppers pay monthly for priority placement in the discovery feed, advanced analytics showing which items are trending, and the verified Pro badge unlocking higher-value client requests. Predictable monthly recurring revenue independent of transaction volume.

€9
Client Premium · per month

Clients pay for wishlist price alerts, priority shopper matching, duty-free airport mode — alerting shoppers travelling through CDG, LHR and DXB — and unlimited saved shopper favourites. Drives retention and deepens platform dependency.

07 — Launch Plan

How we go from
zero to proof in 12 months.

Build the MVP on Bubble.io — client profiles with measurements, shopper profiles with boutique access, the sourcing request flow, photo approval, Stripe escrow, and ID verification.
